About Foz da Cova

Our story, our land, and our vision for regenerative community living.

Our Story

Foz da Cova began in 2019 as a vision to create a living example of regenerative community living in the beautiful countryside of central Portugal. What started as a small group of friends with a shared dream has grown into a thriving community of stewards, learners, and builders.

Our name "Foz da Cova" refers to the natural spring that flows through our land, providing life-giving water to our community and gardens. This spring has been a gathering place for generations, and we continue this tradition by creating a space where people can learn, grow, and connect with nature and each other.

Over the past five years, we've transformed 15 acres of land into a living laboratory for sustainable practices. We've built natural buildings, established organic gardens, designed water systems, and created a supportive community where people can learn practical skills while contributing to something meaningful.

Our Land

Our 15-acre property is nestled in the rolling hills of central Portugal, surrounded by oak forests and traditional farmland. The land features diverse microclimates, from sunny south-facing slopes perfect for gardens to shaded valleys ideal for forest gardens.

Natural Features

  • Natural spring providing year-round water
  • Mixed oak and pine forest
  • South-facing slopes for solar gain
  • Rich soil in valley areas
  • Diverse wildlife and bird species
  • Traditional stone terraces

Infrastructure

  • Natural buildings and cob structures
  • Solar power system
  • Gravity-fed water system
  • Community kitchen and dining area
  • Workshop and tool storage
  • Composting toilets

Location & Access

Foz da Cova is located in central Portugal, approximately 2 hours from Lisbon and 1.5 hours from Porto. We're situated in a rural area with easy access to local villages and towns.

Getting Here

  • By Car: We're accessible by car with parking available on-site
  • By Public Transport: Bus connections from nearby towns
  • By Train: Nearest station is 30 minutes away
