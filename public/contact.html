Location & Directions

Foz da Cova is located in the beautiful countryside of central Portugal, approximately 2 hours from Lisbon and 1.5 hours from Porto.

Getting Here

1

By Car

We're accessible by car with parking available on-site. Exact directions will be provided upon application approval.

2

By Public Transport

Bus connections are available from nearby towns. We can help coordinate pickup from the nearest bus stop.

3

By Train

The nearest train station is 30 minutes away. We can arrange pickup from the station for approved visitors.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to join?

Fill out our application form on the Join Us page. We'll review your application and schedule an interview call to discuss your interest and answer questions.

Can I visit before applying?

We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us in advance to arrange your visit and discuss your interest in our community.

What should I bring?

Bring work clothes, sturdy shoes, personal toiletries, sleeping bag, and any specific items you need. We provide basic accommodation and meals.

How long can I stay?

We prefer stays of 2 weeks or longer, with longer commitments encouraged. This allows for meaningful contribution and learning.
