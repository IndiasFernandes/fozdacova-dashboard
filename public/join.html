Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

Anyone with a genuine interest in community living, sustainability, and helping out is welcome to apply. We value openness, respect, and a willingness to learn. No specific skills are required - we'll teach you what you need to know!

What is the minimum stay?

WWOOFers: 3 weeks minimum (exceptions possible for special circumstances). Stewards: 1â€“3 months is ideal for building relationships and understanding the community.

What do I get in return?

Free food (vegetarian/vegan), comfortable accommodation, access to our beautiful natural setting, learning opportunities, and the chance to be part of a caring community. See the role descriptions above for specific benefits.

What should I bring?

Comfortable work clothes, sturdy shoes, personal items, and an open mind! We provide bedding and basic amenities. If you have specific tools or skills you'd like to share, feel free to bring them.

How do I get there?

We're located in central Portugal, accessible by train to nearby stations. We'll provide detailed directions once you're accepted. We can also arrange pickup from the nearest station.

What's the daily schedule like?

Typically 4-6 hours of work per day, 5 days a week. The rest of the time is yours to explore, relax, or work on personal projects. We have community meals and regular check-ins to ensure everyone feels supported.
